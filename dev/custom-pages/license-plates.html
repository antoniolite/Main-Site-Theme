How to Order a Black & Gold Plate:

  1. Go to the nearest county tag office (specialty tags must be ordered in person)
  2. Request the new UCF specialty plate (Personalized UCF tags are also available)
  3. Pay the $25 annual fee with a smile because you're helping UCF students *Other local and state taxes and fees apply
  4. Watch your mailbox for delivery
  5. Knight Your Ride!
